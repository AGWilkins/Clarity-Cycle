Here's a real drill

Day 3 — Drift Detection

Sit normally for five minutes.

Whenever your mind wanders simply notice it.

Internally say:

"Drift."

Don't pull your attention back. Don't judge yourself. Just notice.

Why?

Most people don't realise they've drifted until several minutes later.

This drill trains the moment of noticing. That moment is where better decisions begin.
